Everyday I'm Hustlin' 25/8


If you know me, you know work is my priority. Why? I love what I do. I love to keep busy, I love to connect with people and I love growth. Works brings out the best in me. I take advantage of most, if not all, opportunities presented to me so I never miss a chance to improve myself.

All the 348,393,930 short and long terms jobs I’ve had in the last 10 years have always taught me something. I've learned how to adapt to different work environments, how to work with people of all background and personalities and most importantly, I have learned how important it is to network.

Here’s a glimpse of my 7-day schedule:

Monday through Friday I work remote as a part time Performance Marketing Coordinator for a marketing agency.

I use my lunch breaks to work on demidiaz.com.

During most evenings and on the weekend, I fit in what I am notorious for... my side hustles. Whether it's bartending a corporate party, demonstrating products at trade show, checking in guests at music festivals or handing out giveaways at Times Square, I keep myself busy with work.

Demi, what about work-life balance? I’m working on it. My work-life balance is chaotic but I wouldn’t have it any other way, for now.
