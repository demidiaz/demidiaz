My Story


Demi Diaz is a true New Yorker; a born and bred go-getter.

From a young age, she has always demonstrated an entrepreneurial spirit. At the ripe age of 5 years old, her neighbors would come over to get pampered in her imaginary beauty business with her imaginary employees. As she grew older, she would join her family at the social club, Centro Cultural Deportivo Dominicano, and would pass the time pushing down soap and handing out paper towels in the women's bathroom for tips. While other kids spent their summers at camp, watching TV or playing video games, Demi was at her father's softball games in Randall's Island selling homemade treats and water. All of this before she actually received her legal working papers at the age of 14.

She has always found a way.

Her hustler spirit followed her throughout her educational career. She continued to refine her creative marketing skills at Fordham University as the Public Relations Coordinator for the socio-cultural club, El Grito de Lares. There, she dabbled into planning and promotions curating events at the Rose Hill campus. Her drive and determination to succeed has since then afforded her incredible opportunities with companies like Sprinkles Cupcakes, Def Jam Records, Roc Nation, Tidal, Chase, Coca-Cola, and SONY.

When she's not working as a Performance Marketing Coordinator or Brand Representative, she is cultivating her own, Young Workaholics - continuing to share the blueprint on the hustle.
